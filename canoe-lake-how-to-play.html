HOW TO PLAY

1. Use Investigate Mode to collect clues. Open the Camera View page and scan the QR codes.

2. If the live camera does not work, scan with your phone's native camera app.

3. Move to Solve Mode, choose a chapter, then drag the correct Fragments into place.

4. If you picked the correct Fragments for that chapter, arrange them in the sequence that matches the events in the story.

5. Solve all three chapters to finish the mystery.

If you get stuck, return to Investigate Mode and replay the audio evidence before trying the chapter again.
